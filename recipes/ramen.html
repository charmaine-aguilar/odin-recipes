Ponyo's Umami Ham Ramen

Description

A magically cooked ramen topped with Ponyo's obession, ham, eggs, and onion chives to warm up Kosuke and Ponyo's aquatic adventure.

Ingredients

  • Instant ramen
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 2 giant slices of ham
  • 1 hard-boiled egg
  • onion chives

Steps

Prepare the magic toppings

  1. Melt butter and set aside.
  2. On a plate or on the lid that goes with your ramen bowl, pour the butter. This will act as adhesive for your toppings.
  3. Carefully place cooked ham, sliced hard-boiled egg and chives.
  4. Refrigerate to bind toppings onto the lid. This will be your ramen's magic!

Cooking the ramen

  1. Get your favorite instant ramen and place the noodles on a bowl.
  2. Sprinkle the seasoning underneath the noodle
  3. Pour hot water on the bowl and close it with the magic lid you have prepared.
  4. Wait a few minutes for the noodles to cook and melt the butter that binds the toppings. This will transfer the toppings on top of the ramen.
  5. Lift the lid and enjoy your customer's expression as they witness the magic happen before their eyes!
