Satsuki's Shishamo Bento

Description

This simple homemade bento made with shishamo, umeboshi, edamame, and sakura denbu will surely fill up anyone's stomach and heart after a long half day of work (assuming you eat it for lunch).

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 umeboshi (pickled Japanese plum)
  • 1 shishamo (small river fish native to Hokkaido)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • handful frozon edamame
  • 1 tbsp sakura denbu (dried and shredded cod flakes)

Steps

  1. Put cooked rice in your bento box about 3/4 of the way full.
  2. Take 1 umeboshi and put in on one side of the rice.
  3. In a small pan, heat the oil on medium-high and cook one shishamo on both sides till cooked.
  4. Place cooked shishamo across the middle of the bento box on the rice.
  5. Boil water and put a handful of frozen edamame according to its package instructions.
  6. Take out the edamame beans out of its pods once cooked and arrange them on the rice.
  7. Sprinkle sakura denbu beside the edamame.
  8. Put a lid on the bento or serve and enjoy!
